Science Laboratory Rules

  • No student is allowed inside a laboratory at any time unless a teacher is present

  • Hands off gas taps

  • No food, sweets, or water is to be consumed in a laboratory

  • Hang bags in bag shelves where they cannot be tripped over, this includes storing coats, blazers, and scarves. Cluttered floors lead to accidents.
  • Wear eye protection and lab coats for all experiments. Eye protection worn on the forehead does not protect the eyes.
  • Stand up when carrying out practical work, so that you can move away quickly if there is an accident
  • Tie or pin back long hair, ties or scarves when carrying out practical work
  • Accidents, however slight, must be reported to your teacher and hands must be washed after working with chemicals and biological specimens.
  • Leave the laboratory clean and tidy. Do not run in the laboratory
